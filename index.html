About Us

Professional Property Management You Can Trust

AHUVIM LLC is a professionally managed rental management company dedicated to delivering reliable, efficient, and high-quality property management solutions. Based in Chesapeake, Virginia, we specialize in overseeing residential rental properties while providing exceptional service to both property owners and tenants.

Founded on August 23, 2024, AHUVIM LLC was established with the goal of creating well-maintained, welcoming, and professionally managed rental communities. As a Limited Liability Company, we operate with integrity, accountability, and professionalism.

Residential Rental Management
